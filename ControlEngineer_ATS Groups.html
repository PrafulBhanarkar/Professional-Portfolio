Material Handling & Skid Transport

Asian Paints' Visakhapatnam facility is recognized as one of the world's most highly automated smart paint manufacturing plants. Working under ATS, our controls engineering team deployed smart conveying networks that orchestrated container logistics, raw material routing, and automated container baking/drying runs.

Core Engineering Solutions Delivered:

Asian Paints Vizag — Control Specs

PLC System: Siemens SIMATIC S7-1500 Fail-safe CPU
HMI Environment: WinCC SCADA Advanced / Unified Panels
Industrial Networks: PROFINET RT (Real-Time) ring topology for high reliability
VFD Architecture: SEW Eurodrive Movidrive frequency inverters
Actuator / Sensor: Sick Laser Scanners, Pepperl+Fuchs photoelectric grids

The Engineering Challenge: In a paint production environment, conveyor operations have zero margin for error. A sudden stop can lead to chemical settling or paint curing failures inside curing ovens.

Delivered Solution: We created localized conveyor segment overrides. In the event of a downstream line blockage, upstream skillet conveyors automatically switch to a low-speed accumulator mode. This keeps the active paint ovens moving while safely slowing the production lines, avoiding oven baking errors and saving thousands in potential material waste.

FIAT Ranjangaon — Control Specs

PLC System: Rockwell Automation ControlLogix 5580
Fieldbus Protocol: Profibus DP and EtherNet/IP Industrial Loop
Safety Class: SIL 3 / PL e (ISO 13849-1) functional safety loops
Skillet Elevators: Demag vertical hoist scissor lifts with resolver feedback
EMS Carriers: Dual-drive monorail trolley with localized safety blocks

The Engineering Challenge: Automotive trim lines require variable conveyor spacing and heights. To ensure workers can safely and ergonomically mount engines, doors, and interiors, skillet conveyances must raise and lower car bodies smoothly while in motion.

Delivered Solution: Commissioned laser-range distance meters on scissors lifts. Built synchronized speed profile blocks inside the PLC logic, guaranteeing that even when a vehicle is moving at full line speed, the vertical adjustment occurs perfectly level without sway. Configured safety light screens at all pinch points, stopping operations instantly if an operator crosses the barrier envelope.
